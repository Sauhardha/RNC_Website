A Nepalese community that shares joy & support.

Our organisation was established to preserve our culture with residents living in the Riverina area. We aim to pass our traditions to the younger generations & also support newcoming residents with their endeavours.

Tradition

One of our main goals is to preserve our traditions and to maintain them amongst the younger generations. We also host activities & opportunities to learn more about Nepalese heritage.

Support

We're in this together, always ready to share our knowledge & guidance with members. This initiative also extends to arrange support or relief in the case of emergencies or disasters in Australia, Nepal & other countries.

Celebration

Festivals are at the heart of Nepalese culture. Each year we celebrate major events like Dashain, Tihar, Teej & more; keeping the Nepali spirit alive.

Connection

Our network of Nepalese members are able to share their experience for support. Particularly for new residents - it can be daunting settling into a new area, do not worry, we are by your side.
